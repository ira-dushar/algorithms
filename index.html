Course is starting from October 9th. You are welcome to come!


What is it? Free offline course. We are discussing algorithms there. A lot of code assignments and discussions expected. Basic knowledge of at least one programming language required. All code examples will be shown in Python, but you are free to use any programming language for your tasks.

Who is the lecturer? Ira Dushar

When? Each Tuesday and Thursday at 19:30. Starting from October 9th for the next 2 months. Saturday - optional meetup.

Where? Kyiv, Peremohy Ave, 37, Library of Igor Sikorsky Kyiv Polytechnic Institute, 2nd floor, Student Space Belka

What else? This is not a typical college course. There are no exams, no marks, no control whatsoever. As we will do coding sessions there, it's highly recommended to bring a laptop to meetups. All questions and announcements will go via our Slack channel.

How to join? Just fill the registration form. Link to the form is attached to the #general channel in our Slack group.
